About Think Culture Foundation

Think Culture Foundation is a not-for-profit organization which believes in promoting harmony amongst civilizations through the promotion of arts and culture.

As an organization, we are committed towards the propagation of global cultural knowledge to help empower youth, children and women for a better tomorrow, together.

At TCF, we believe that only by nurturing the soil today can we expect blossoming flowers tomorrow.

Mission and Vision

Mission
To empower youth with cultural understanding by promoting meaningful interactions with arts and culture

Vision
To enable cordial cross-cultural human interactions

The Idea Behind

Understanding culture and cultural differences is a step towards accepting and respecting other cultures
How do we define culture?
“The arts & other manifestations of human intellectual achievement regarded collectively”
--------
It is when we make efforts to understand other cultures and interact with them through a human lens, that we begin our journey towards appreciating them

Lack of cultural awareness and understanding is leading to propagation of negative cultural stereotypes in the society.

An extreme form of community hatred germinates from this lack of cultural understanding, sometimes even leading to wars

70% international ventures fail because of cultural differences and employees’ inability to understand each other’s culture

Education, tourism, humanity - all institutions are suffering because of this prevailing lack of cultural understanding in the society

Impact – Cultural Understanding

1

Propagates an open mindset & helps in minimizing stereotypes

2

Increases trust and cooperation, taking a step towards world peace

3

Promotes inclusion and equality

4

Increases creativity in problem-solving through new perspectives, ideas, and strategies
